FAQ

Common Questions

Which dataloggers are supported?+
We support MHD, Bootmod3, EcuTek, HP Tuners, COBB, AEM and any CSV with standard headers.
Can I compare multiple logs side-by-side?+
Yes. Pro users can overlay multiple runs to see exactly how map changes or hardware upgrades affected performance curves.
Is my tuning data private?+
Yes. Your logs are private to your account. We never share or sell any tune configurations.
Do you support custom PID mapping?+
Our proprietary parser handles 400+ standard PIDs. Custom PIDs can be mapped via our Tuner workspace API.
Does the AI Tuner replace a human?+
No. It's a second pair of eyes that correlates data faster than a human, but map changes should always be verified by a professional.
Is there a mobile app?+
We are a mobile-first web app. You can upload and analyze logs directly from your phone's browser at the track.
